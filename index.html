How to Play

Guess the secret 4-digit number in 8 tries.

Each guess must be 4 unique digits from 1–9 (no zeros, no repeats).

After each guess you'll see two clues:

Digits — how many of your digits appear anywhere in the answer.
Positions — how many digits are in the exact right spot.

Example — secret number is 3 8 1 5
